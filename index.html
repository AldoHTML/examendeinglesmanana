Comparativos y superlativos faster / the fastest · 50 formas
⌄
📌 Regla rápida: Adjetivos cortos (1 sílaba): añade -er / -est. Adjetivos largos (2+ sílabas): usa more / the most. Irregulares: memorízalos. Comparativo = entre 2 cosas · Superlativo = el extremo del grupo.
Comparativo
entre 2
adj corto + -er + than → faster than, bigger than
more + adj largo + than → more beautiful than
Superlativo
el extremo
the + adj corto + -est → the fastest, the biggest
the most + adj largo → the most beautiful
Regla ortog.
ortografía
CVC corta → dobla consonante: big→bigger · hot→hotter
Termina en -y → cambia a -i: happy→happier · easy→easiest
Irregulares — memorizar obligatorio
good → better → the best bueno → mejor → el mejor "She is a good student, but her sister is better. Her brother is the best in the class."
bad → worse → the worst malo → peor → el peor "Monday is bad, but Tuesday is worse. Friday is the worst day for traffic."
far → farther / further → the farthest / furthest lejos → más lejos → el más lejos "He lives far, but she lives farther. That village is the farthest from the city."
little → less → the least poco → menos → el menos "I have little time, she has less. He has the least free time of all."
many / much → more → the most mucho(s) → más → el más / la mayoría "She has many friends, but he has more. They have the most followers."
Adjetivos cortos — comparativo (-er) y superlativo (-est)
fast → faster → the fastest rápido → más rápido → el más rápido "Trains are faster than buses. The airplane is the fastest option."
slow → slower → the slowest lento → más lento → el más lento "Reading is slower than watching a video. Walking is the slowest way to travel."
big → bigger → the biggest grande → más grande → el más grande "A dog is bigger than a cat. An elephant is the biggest land animal."
small → smaller → the smallest pequeño → más pequeño → el más pequeño "A phone is smaller than a laptop. A coin is the smallest object in my bag."
tall → taller → the tallest alto → más alto → el más alto "She is taller than her brother. Burj Khalifa is the tallest building in the world."
short → shorter → the shortest corto/bajo → más corto → el más corto "Summer days are shorter in December. January has the shortest daylight in winter."
long → longer → the longest largo → más largo → el más largo "This road is longer than the other. The Nile is the longest river in the world."
hot → hotter → the hottest caliente/caluroso → más caliente → el más caliente "Today is hotter than yesterday. July is the hottest month in Puebla."
cold → colder → the coldest frío → más frío → el más frío "The north is colder than the south. Antarctica is the coldest place on Earth."
old → older → the oldest viejo/mayor → más viejo → el más viejo "My dad is older than my mom. My grandfather is the oldest person in the family."
young → younger → the youngest joven → más joven → el más joven "She is younger than her classmates. He is the youngest member of the team."
new → newer → the newest nuevo → más nuevo → el más nuevo "This phone is newer than mine. That model is the newest on the market."
cheap → cheaper → the cheapest barato → más barato → el más barato "Buses are cheaper than taxis. The market is the cheapest place to buy food."
hard → harder → the hardest difícil/duro → más difícil → el más difícil "Math is harder than English for me. Chemistry is the hardest subject this year."
bright → brighter → the brightest brillante/luminoso → más brillante → el más brillante "LED lights are brighter than regular bulbs. The sun is the brightest star we see."
strong → stronger → the strongest fuerte → más fuerte → el más fuerte "Steel is stronger than wood. Titanium is the strongest common metal."
weak → weaker → the weakest débil → más débil → el más débil "The signal is weaker upstairs. The basement has the weakest connection."
safe → safer → the safest seguro → más seguro → el más seguro "The bus is safer than a motorcycle. Flying is the safest way to travel."
clean → cleaner → the cleanest limpio → más limpio → el más limpio "This room is cleaner than the other. The kitchen is the cleanest place in the house."
dark → darker → the darkest oscuro → más oscuro → el más oscuro "The forest is darker than the city. Midnight is the darkest hour."

Estructuras útiles con comparativos
as + adj + as tan … como (igualdad) "She is as tall as her brother." / "This test is not as hard as the last one."
much + comparativo mucho más … (énfasis en la diferencia) "Exercising is much better than watching TV all day."
a little + comparativo un poco más … (diferencia pequeña) "Today is a little colder than yesterday."
one of the + superlativo + plural noun uno de los más … (superlativo en grupo) "Puebla is one of the most beautiful cities in Mexico."
the + comparativo, the + comparativo cuanto más … más … (relación proporcional) "The more you practice, the better you become."

Estructura del párrafo 3 partes obligatorias
⌄
1. Topic sentence
Idea central del párrafo. Sangría al inicio. No da detalles, solo anuncia el tema."It is easy to stay healthy if you follow some simple steps."
2. Body (min. 3)
First, … + detalle.
Also / Next, … + detalle.
Another reason … + detalle.
3. Concluding sentence
Repite la idea con otras palabras. Sin info nueva. Adjetivo diferente."These simple steps can help anyone live a healthier life."
Fórmula oración de apoyo:
Conector + sujeto + verbo + complemento + adjetivo
First, eating fruits every day is an excellent habit.
Fórmula concluding sentence:
Retoma tema + reflexión final (sin nueva información)
These are the reasons why staying healthy is not complicated.
💡 Usa pronombres (he, she, they, them) para no repetir el mismo sustantivo. Usa posesivos (my, her, their) para mostrar pertenencia.
